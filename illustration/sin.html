"Sin & Sinuous," 2024

Sin & Sinuous is a series of eight short stories that follows a seemingly invisible high school student, Aurora.
Aurora lives her life on the sidelines, observing those in the spotlight. In the shadows, Aurora feels free to pass judgement on her peers, and decides takes the punishment of those who "have it too easy" into her own hands.
